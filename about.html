About

Not Too Sweet is a home bakery featuring pastries and desserts with modern fusion-style and time-tested
traditional recipes, handmade using local and organic ingredients. Not Too Sweet is run by self-taught
baker Levin Gong. Beginning to bake from the age of 12, he continues to learn, experiment, and hone his
technique in the kitchen. Now an engineering student at University of Washington, Seattle, Levin
continues to bake out of his apartment at school and back home in San Jose, California. Outside of
baking, Levin can often be found in the gym training Olympic-style weightlifting and mixed martial arts.
